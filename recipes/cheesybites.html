CHEESY BITES

Description

Wow... yummy... cheesy... and crispy starter !!!!!!!!!

Here, i would like to represent yummy starter with salted biscuits.

Ingredients

packet salted biscuits

2 tbps butter

2 tbps tomato (finally chopped)

2 tbps onion (finally chopped)

2 tbps capsicum (finally chopped)

1 tbps tomato ketchup

2 tbps grated cheese>

Pinch black paper powder

Coriander leaves (for garnish)

1 tbps green chutney ( pest of green chilies, ginger and garlic)

1 tbps boiled corn

1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Take a thick pan, heat butter and add onion, capsicum, tomato on it and saute for few minutes.
  2. Sprinkle black paper powder, sugar and boiled corn in it and mix it properly and saute for few seconds.
